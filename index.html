It's diggin' time! Beta

The local op-shop is just about to close and you've got just 30 seconds to look through the crates.

You can only afford 5 records, so choose wisely!

What is this?

Just a dumb little game that lets you experience looking for records in an Australian op-shop, without having to subject yourself to the actual disappointment of doing it in real life.

I also do some other record-related stuff.
